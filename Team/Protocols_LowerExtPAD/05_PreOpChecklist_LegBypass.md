For surgical bypasses of the legs, all iMed consents should have 1+ of:
- Artery – Lower Extremity Bypass _(most common choice for fem-pop, fem-tibial, other infrainguinal bypasses)_
- Aorta – Aortobifemoral Bypass Surgery
- Artery – Femoral-Femoral Bypass
- Artery – Axillofemoral Bypass


**All three** of following should also be included as possible:
1. Artery – Endarterectomy and Patch Angioplasty of Lower Extremity
2. Vein – Harvesting (Open)
3. Artery – Angiogram with possible interventions (Peripheral)

Additional pre-op checklist itmes
[ ] Type & cross order for 2u pRBCs.  
[ ] Pre-op chlorhexidine + nasal swab bundle received.  
[ ] Antifungal cream for any yeast colonization in inguinal area.
