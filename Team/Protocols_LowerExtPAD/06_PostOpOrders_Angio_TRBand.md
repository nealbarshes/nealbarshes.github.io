Text orders: 

"Place oxygen saturation probe on thumb or index finger of access site hand to confirm patent hemostasis while patient is in Post-Anesthesia Recovery Unit (PACU). Call staff surgeon or senior resident involved in the case or OR 8A (ext 222761) if oxygen saturation readings from the access site hand are <93%. 

"Beginning 10 minutes after arrival to PACU: remove 3mL of air from the TR Band every 10 minutes. 

"If bleeding is seen during deflation/removal, insert 1-3mL more air to restore hemostasis and ensure oxygen saturation readings from access site hand are <93%. Wait 30 minutes, then resume removal of 3mL of air every 10 minutes.

"Once air has been completely removed from band, confirm bleeding has stopped. Unfasten the adjustable band while 
stabilizing access site with gentle pressure. Remove band by lifting slowly toward palm of hand.  Apply sterile dressing.
