------------------------------------------------
NECK HEMATOMA
------------------------------------------------
IMMEDIATELY examine patient, eval. airway
 ==> call rapid response/code blue FIRST if unstable
 ==> otherwise discuss findings with staff, senior 

Sit patient up in med. 
Add telemetry with continuous O2 monitoring.
Hold prophylactic dose Lovenox or heparin (though continue aspirin).
Change diet to NPO.



------------------------------------------------
HEADACHE
------------------------------------------------
Evaluate for hyperperfusion syndrome; 
  control blood pressure to avoid hemorrhagic stroke.

Talk to the patient: rate headache severity, ask about visual changes.
Examine the patient: neuro exam for asymmetric changes.

Speak to nurse about importance of blood pressure control.

Change parameter on existing PRN antihypertensives from 140mmHg to 120mmHg.
Add additional PRN antihypertensives; consider 5A/S
