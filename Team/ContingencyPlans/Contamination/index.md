### During the case:
1. Swab the surgical field, the contaminated/suspicious instrument(s), and other instruments in the set or table.  
2. Irrigate with antibiotic solution.  
3. Consider additional intravenous antibiotics.  

### After the operation:
4. Inform the patient and/or family.  
5. Brief other staff surgeons and OR nursing.  
6. JPSR.
